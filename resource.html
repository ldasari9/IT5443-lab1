Person/Thing Relationship to Apple
Steve Jobs Co-founder of Apple Inc. and key visionary behind the development of iconic products like the iPhone and iPad.
Tim Cook Current CEO of Apple Inc., succeeding Steve Jobs in 2011.
Jony Ive Former Chief Design Officer at Apple, played a crucial role in the design of Apple's iconic products.
iOS The mobile operating system developed by Apple, powering devices like the iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch.
macOS The desktop operating system developed by Apple for Mac computers.
iPhone Apple's flagship smartphone, known for its sleek design and advanced features.
Apple Store Apple's retail stores worldwide, offering a wide range of Apple products and services.
Apple Music Apple's music streaming service, providing access to a vast catalog of songs and exclusive content.
Apple Watch Apple's smartwatch, offering health and fitness tracking, communication features, and app integration.
Apple Pay Apple's mobile payment and digital wallet service, allowing secure transactions using Apple devices.
